About

I'm a citizen of the world who loves to listen to, tell, and share stories.

My journey as a project coordinator delivering high quality experiences with and for diverse communities began over fifteen years ago. I have led teams and collaborated with teenagers, mum’s and bub’s, adults of all ages and abilities, professional artists, and people from different cultural backgrounds.

From one on one mentoring to production management of large scale outdoor events, I bring my capacity for lateral thinking, problem solving, interpersonal skills, and creative vision.

I’m a bush walker and nature lover. There’s nothing I like more than jumping into a fresh river, lake, or ocean somewhere in the world.
